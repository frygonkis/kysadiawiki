Void

The Void is a mysterious and enigmatic realm that exists beyond the boundaries of the normal world. It is a place of darkness and emptiness, where the elements composing it become simpler the further you get from an aurabranch. Life can't exist in the void and its secrets have fascinated philosophers, scientists, and mystics for centuries.

Structure

The Void is a vast and featureless expanse of nothingness, stretching out infinitely in all directions. Near the edges of the Void, where it meets the normal world, there are areas of transition where the Void and the physical world meet. These areas are known as boundaries, and they are characterized by aetheric energy that flows between the Void and the normal world.

The void gets simpler the further away it is from an aurabranch. Near the border between the normal world and the void, the void is characterized by a specific darkness and by a lack of geographic features such as mountains or rivers. Oceans and landmasses still exist at this point. If you get even further, the dirt becomes one element and it is hard to walk through. The air is dense and the oceans start to mix up with the landmasses. Even further, there are only 2 elements left that start to mix with one another, air and a mix of water and dirt.

As one moves deeper into the Void, the landscape becomes simpler and simpler, until there is only one element: a featureless, black void. There are no geographical features, no creatures, and no light. It is a place of complete emptiness.>

The Void and Kysadia

The Void has a significant influence on the world of Kysadia. The aurabranches that connect the normal world to the Void are a source of aetheric energy that is essential for the survival of the world's ecosystems. The energy that flows through the void can be harnessed by skilled void mages to power technology, enhance their abilities, and access mystical powers. However, the Void also poses a threat to Kysadia. When an aurabranch collapses, it can cause devastating destruction to the surrounding area, and the aetheric energy can be released in dangerous, unpredictable ways. There could also be void related natural catastrophes that cause a threat to both the people and the flora and fauna. This phonomena, such as void storms, void quakes and void incursions can cause big destruction.

Study and Theories

The Void has long been a subject of speculation and theory among scholars and mystics. Some believe that the Void is the source of all existence, while others see it as a place of darkness and chaos. Some have even proposed that the Void is home to beings of immense power and knowledge, who observe and manipulate the normal worlds from afar.

The void is also a huge topic of science of Kysadia. Various sciences have developed studying many topics. The most famous sciences are:

  • voidology - the science that studies the void overall. This science is based on experimental philosophy because the depths of the void are unaccessible and the voidologists have to, based on observations and experiments, deduce topics about what the void is and what's its origin.
  • voidengineering - this science studies the effect of the void on infrastructure in kysadia. Their goals include creating safer houses and storages near the void and creating void proof materials
  • voidbiology - this science studies the effects of the void on kysadia's flora and fauna and how they evolve to counter the void. They also try implementing flora to the void for experimental reasons
  • voidmeteorology - this science studies how the void affects the weather in kysadia. The most important thing they do is predicting void related catastrophes, that can be predicted with some days in advance. Therefore, most voidmeteorologists are void mages that can feel the interference of the void phenomenon with the void fabric


Despite centuries of study, the true nature of the Void remains a mystery. Some speculate that it may hold the key to unlocking the secrets of the universe, while others fear that its dark influence could bring about the end of all things.

Conclusion

The Void is a realm of mystery and darkness, with a profound influence on the world of Kysadia. Its aetheric energy is essential for the survival of life on the world, but it also poses a significant threat to its civilizations. Despite centuries of study, the Void remains a subject of speculation and theory, with its true nature and purpose remaining shrouded in mystery.
